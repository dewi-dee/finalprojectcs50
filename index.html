About Me

Computer sience knowledge is something that must be owned by the younger generation today, even in the future era. This is because the digital era continues to develop rapidly along with the emergence of new trends in the world of technology, especially since almost various activities can be run virtually.

Computers and other technological tools have become a staple that cannot be separated from the daily activities of society. it is very important for students to learn computer science as life skills. today, almost every job vacancy provides requirements regarding the ability to use computers. in another case, there are so many things we can do to earn money with computer skills. in other words, skill of utilizing computer science must be one of the provisions for students to face their future.

Because of this awareness, the government through the Ministry of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia has made it mandatory to return to Informatics subjects from elementary to secondary education.

The ministry of education of the republic of Indonesia collaborated with Harvard University in the CS50x Indonesian Teacher pilot program, as one of the government's supports in improving the digital capabilities of Indonesian teachers, especially in the field of informatics.
